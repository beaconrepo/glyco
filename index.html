Chemistry in Every Trade

Welcome to Kuwait Chemical House Company

Kuwait Chemical House Company (KCHC) is a large business trade house engaged in trading of all kinds of chemicals since the last few decades. We have a considerable business volume with various government and private sectors in the state of Kuwait and entire GCC States, through supply agreements, purchase orders, letters of understanding and services contracts.

We cater to the needs of various chemical services and are also looking forward to expand our horizon and establish a long-lasting business relationship with our esteemed customers.
